Cases

Cases play a big role in computer performance due to the fact that it is a major source of PC cooling. You always wanna make sure your computer has good airflow to make sure the all the parts stay cool. Parts that dont have sufficient cooling will quickly deteriorate in performance. They come in many form factors from ATX to Mini ATX.

Entry Level

A great performing case for entry level builds and tight budgets.

CM MasterBox Q300L
The CoolerMaster MasterBox is a great budget case that provides great performance. It features a mesh front panel which makes sure your parts stay cool. You can show off parts with the transparent acrylic side panel making sure your PC stands out on your desk. With a modular I/O panel, you can place it on any side of the case you desire.
Mid End

For those who want both an asthetically good looking and very well performing case.

CORSAIR 4000D Airflow
The Corsair 4000D comes in both black and white to fit your needs. It provides plenty of airflow due to the high airflow front panel giving you maximum cooling. The 4000D is equipped with CORSAIR RAPIDROUTE cable management to make sure you build looks clean. The 4000D is equipped with 2 120mm AirGuide fans to concentrate airflow and enhance cooling.
High End

A very popular and aesthetically pleasing choice. Typically for those who plan on having a water cooler.

Lian Li O11 Dynamic
The O11 Dynamic is a very popular choice for high end PC builds. The O11 features a dual chamber design to make sure your computer gets the best cooling and aesthetics possible. The O11 dynamic has tons of space for upgrades so theres no need to worry about changing cases in the future. It is recommended to use an AIO cooler for this case.
