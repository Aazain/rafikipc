NIC

An NIC or Network Interface Controller is a device that is plugged typically into a PCIe slot on the motherboard to make a computer capable of connecting to a WiFi network or an Ethernet connection. A NIC is a good choice for those who dont plan on using a motherboard that doesnt support an internet connection.

Low Speed

A good choice for people with network speeds under 150Mbps

Rosewill N150
The N150 is a great choice for people who have a network speed of under 150Mbps. Its a cheap network card that can connect to 2.4GHz through a normal 2.0 or newer USB port for a price of $9.99
High Speed

For people with high speed internet connections planning to use WiFi with their computers.

Rosewill PCIe WiFi Card
This Rosewill PCIe network card is a dual band network card that can connect to networks with bands up to 5.8GHz. This card also allows your computer to enable its bluetooth feature which is great for people who want to use wireless products with their computer.
Insane speeds

For anyone who wants fast speed ethernet with insane speeds.

Asus XG-C100C
The Asus XG network card is a PCIe ethernet adapter that lets you reach ethernet speeds of up to 10Gbps. This is great for people who require fast online data transfer speeds or need fast connections for lower latency with gaming or livestreaming. This card does not support WiFi which means it isn't a good idea for those who want a wireless internet connection.
