Graphics Cards

The Graphics Card also reffered to as the GPU is a very important part in the computer, especially for gamers. The GPU handles all of the output sent to your screen and handles all the rendering. The better the GPU, the higher the FPS and you always want to have high framrates to enjoy your video games.

Entry Level

A great budget GPU for casual gamers.

Nvidia GeForce GTX 1660 OC
With a 1830MHz boost clock, 8002MHz memory clock and 6GB of VRAM, the 1660 is a great GPU for people who want to play video games casually. The 1660 is great for playing AAA titles and tackling entry level content creation.
Mid End

A GPU for people who want to experience games at high framerates.

Nvidea GeForce RTX 2070 Super
The RTX 2070s is a great GPU for those who want a visually stunning experience at high framerates. The RTX 2070s features ray tracing for immersive experiences. The 2070s comes with a 1770MHz boost clock aned a 14000MHz memory clock with 8GB GDDR6 VRAM. The 2070s is great choice for gaming and any other 3D application.
High End

The best Graphics Cards you can get. High framerates, great aesthetics, and the best performance you can get.

Nvidia GeForce RTX 3090
The 3090 is equipped with a whopping 24GB of GDDR6X VRAM, a 1695MHz boost and 19500MHz memory clock. This beast is the best GPU you can get with support for 8k gaming. Powered by Nvidea Ampere, the 3090 provides the best AI performance and enhances Ray Tracing cores. The 3090 can handle any game at ultra settings without trouble. This is the best GPU you can get for any sort of content creation, media design, or gaming title.
