Peripherals

Its important to make sure that the peripherals you choose are not only good but what suits you. A peripheral can be cheaper than another, but the cheaper peripheral can also be better than the expensive one. Preference is on of the biggest factors. These are some recommended peripherals.

Headset

A recommended headset that provides great audio with great bass, mids and highs.

Logitech g635
The g635 is a great headset with amazing sound quality. The headset features a 7.1 surround sound feature which can be anbled in the Logitech GHUB app. The headset comes with a 50mm driver which gives punchy bass and pronounced mids and highs. The g635 also stylishly features RGB lights which can be controlled in app.
Mouse

A stylish and accurate device that can become an in game weapon in the right hands.

Glorious Model D
The Glorious Model D is an amazing mouse with style and precesion. Equipped with a pixart 3360 sensor, the Model D insures you hit every shot in game. The shell of the mouse has a honeycomb hole design to cut weight to only 69g. The Model D also comes with a special paracorded cable which is so light that it feels wireless. To top it of there are 2 color options, black and white both equipped with rgb strips that can be controlled via a PC application.
Keyboard

A stylish, compact keyboard that feels great to type on.

GamaKay K87
The K87 is an amazing choice for people who don't know much about keyboards but want the best quality for a price that doesn't break your bank. At $95, you get a rgb keyboard equipped with a hotswappable pcb, acrylic case, detachable usb c port, and an overall quality keyboard. A hotswappable PCB allows you to be able to swap out the switches of your keyboard. If you buy the keyboard with clicky loud switches and get tired of it, you can get different switches that are tactile or linear and swap them in without having to buy a new keyboard. Mechanical keyboards are also a lot more comfortable to type on compared to membrane keyboards a lot of people are used to.
