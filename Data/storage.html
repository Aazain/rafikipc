Storage

Storage is where the computer stores all of its files and where the operating system is running off of. Choosing a good storage device can be hard because there are so many options but that's why we're here to help.

Entry Level

A good option for everyday use, fast boot times, and fast pc responsivness

WD Blue
The WD blue 500gb ssd is a great choice for people who want a fast pc for everyday tasks. It has a read speed of 560MB/s and Write speed of 530MB/s.
Mid End

For those who want ultra fast boot times and faster application/game load times.

WD Black SN750
The WD Black is a 1TB M.2 storage device which is know to be some of the fastest storage devices. The WD Black NVMe has a read speed of 3500MB/s and write speed of 3000MB/s which means everyday tasks will be fast and loading/file transfer speeds will be very fast.
High End

For anyone who wants the fastest storage they can get.

SEAGATE FireCuda 520
The FireCuda is a 1TB NVMe storage device with a whopping read speed of 5000MB/s and write speed of 4400MB/s. This will ensure everything will be loaded in the fastest times possible. This storage device only supports the high speeds if your motherboard also supports Gen4 PCIe.
