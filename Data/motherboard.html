Motherboards

The motherboard plays an important part by taking power supplied by the PSU and giving power to all of the components connected to the PC. This is where all of the parts are put together. Its important to choose a motherboard with the same socket as your processor otherwise your parts may not fit.

Entry Level

A good choice for people who just need their pc to work and dont plan on overclocking.

GIGABYTE B450 AUROS ELITE
The b450 auros elite is a good motherboard for am4 socket cpus. It has good VRMs for its price range and features dual channel ram compatibility. It comes with 4 ram slots, 8 usb slots, and all other basic needed features that you may need. This board does not support WiFi so it is a good idea to get an NIC unless you plan on using an ethernet connection. The board also comes with a M.2 for NVMe storage.
Mid End

For those who want the best value and plan on overclocking.

ASUS ROG B450F
The ROG B450f is a great motherboard for those who plan on overclocking their CPU. It comes with great VRMs which will give you satisfying performance boosts when you overclock your CPU. This motherboard supports AM4 socket CPUs. This board is equipped with dual channel compatibility, 4 ram slots, 2 M.2 slots, 7 USB ports and other ports for ethernet, displays and audio connections.
High End

The choice for those who plan on overclocking and getting the best performance possible.

ASUS ROG CROSSHAIR VIII FORMULA
A great choice for those who plan on overclocking a high end CPU. This board comes with great VRMs for overclocking needs and has the lastest tech for all other parts of your PC. Equipped with a PCIe 4.0 slot, Onboard WiFi, 5Gbps LAN, USB3.2 and multiple M.2 slots, there isn't anything that this board doesn't offer.
