RAM

RAM (Random Access Memory) is like the short term memory for the computer. It temporarily stores information that a device needs. It is a better idea to get fast RAM to make sure your PC doesn't slow down as you use applications. The RAM is super fast to access which improves computer speeds due to the fact that the computer doesn't need to reach into the slower hard disk drives on the PC.

Entry Level

For those who dont require using demanding software.

G.SKILL Aegis
This set of dual channel RAM is good for everyday tasks and light gaming. It features a speed of 2400MHz with a cas latency of 15. The timing on this ram is 15-15-15-35.
Mid End

A good choice for those who play video games or need RAM for demanding software or rendering.

CORSAIR Vengeance RGB PRO
This RAM kit is great for everyday multitasking and heavy gaming. With a dual channel kit of 16GB at 3200MHz and a cas latency of 16, this ram kit will be more than enough under heavy loads. This RAM kit also looks very good inside builds which is always a plus.
High End

For those who want good performance in both productivity and gaming.

G.Skill Trident Z RGB
This RAM kit looks amazing but it is also overkill. This RAM kit is way more than enough for heavy rendering, 3D modelling, content creation, gaming and any other task. This RAM kit features a dual channel setup of 32GB of RAM at 4000MHz at a CL19.
